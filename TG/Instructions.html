Consider the following situation:

Two participants to this study are randomly and anonymously paired. One participant is labelled Subject A and the other Subject B.

Subject A must decide among two alternatives, called Option 1 and Option 2.



  • Option 1: Subject A keeps the 10 Points and nothing is passed to Subject B. The game ends without Subject B taking any decision and earnings will be 10 Points for Subject A and 0 Points for Subject B, respectively.
  • Option 2: Subject A passes 10 Points to Subject B. The 10 Points will be multiplied by a factor of 4, thus Subject B will receive 40 Points. In this case Subject B must decide among Option 3 and Option 4:
  • Option 3: Subject B keeps the 40 Points and nothing is passed back to Subject A. Earnings will be 0 Points for Subject A and 40 Points for Subject B, respectively.
  • Option 4: Subject B keeps the 18 Points and passes back 22 Points to Subject A. Earnings will be 22 Points for Subject A and 18 Points for Subject B, respectively.
One of the participants in each couple is randomly assigned to be Subject A and the other to be Subject B.
